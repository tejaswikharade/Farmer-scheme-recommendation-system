Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)

For smaller size ponds/dug wells, cost will be admissible on pro rata basis 50% of cost and limited to Rs. 0.75 lakhs per beneficiary for plains & Rs. 0.90 lakhs / beneficiary for hilly areas with 300 micron plastic lining/RCC lining, for 2-hectare command area. In case of non-lined ponds 30%, less assistance will be provided.
